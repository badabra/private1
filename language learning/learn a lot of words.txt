words must be learned in sentences/context

1 - learn a 1000 words with a 1000 exemple sentences as fast as possible
- use mnemonics - words insite exemple sentences - giving how it is actually used - the context - also the grammar without studying rules
- takes these sentence with text audio and their translation with them - first you create an association of the new words you are trying to learn - this is called mnemonics - every new words you make a mnemonics association -
- and then you start doing spaced repetition - reading listening and repeating aloud over and over again - with the most common words and also you pick a topic you are really interested in and start learning vocabulary from that specific topic what ever you are interested in

2 - listen to the language everyday - in the beginning you probably would understand nothing or some or none - and in a few weeks you will start hearing words you know as you learn more and more words - that is spaced repetition and pattern recognition

3 - speaking : do shadowing of audio of phrases and make phrases thinking in your target language
- you should start speaking from day one doing shadowing out loud
- have language island you shadowed after having prepared them - phrases you would say in specific situations you think you would arrive in or stories of your day like someone asked you to tell them about your self or about something you know
- all you need is write sentences, and a lot of repetition - reading them , listening to them, repeating them out loud, and doing active recall with them.
- knowing a sentence is if you can recall it automatically from memory when you need it or like modify or build from already know phrases and words

- if you can only recognise and not produce words every time you are in an conversation - it is really frustrating - you need daily practice in your own words - trying thinking in your target language - explaining aloud in it - practicing the vocab and using vowels and consonents and accent of the language
