1 : experience  : cv
what have been the most challenging part of your experience
how has been your experience in this job?

happened/completed in past - meaningful to present 
can be a past completed or not connected to the present -> always present consequenece
2 : news/updates (pretending/being important)
happened in the past with a present consequence on my "life" or "life style" or "on me"/"on you"
3 : actions you are expecting
A : 1500 soldiers died in the Red War (s.p. -> complete -> number fixed)
B : 1500 soldiers have died in the Blue War (P.P. -> Incomplete -> war still going on - didn't end)
 C: I didn't do it
 D: I have not done it,(yet)
4: Success & failures
my son learned how to read : no present consequence - it is a normal thing
my son has learned how to read : completed action with a present consequence : there was a challenge somewhere - now there is success
